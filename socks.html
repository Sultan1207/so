ABOUT US

Our company is created for everyone, especially for those who constantly lose the second
pair of socks. For those whose one-pair socks lie in the dresser, not knowing why they
exist. However, we believe mismatching socks are okay. Our message evolves from
this problem into that it is okay to be different. Our campaign is to use this
message and promote anti-bullying. We give the chance to create socks
that will be hand painted in the shape of the anti-bullying handprint
both different to one another.
